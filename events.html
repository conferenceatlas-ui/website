Atlas II: Transhumanism and Technology

International Conference on Transhumanism and Technology
October 24, 2024
Society of University Professors and Scientists, Zagreb

Organized by Studia Croatica, the Student Association of the Faculty of Croatian Studies, Atlas II brought together leading international scholars to examine transhumanism and technology from a socio-humanistic perspective. The conference explored whether technological development represents opening Pandora's box, leading humanity from limited innocence into a soulless dystopian future of new technological gods. The event maintained Atlas's core ideals: taking responsibility for posing key questions of our time and believing in the meaningfulness of human action in a world that often relegates meaning and principles to the background.

Atlas I: Modernity and Responsibility

International Conference on Modernity and Responsibility
May 23-24, 2023
Hotel Dubrovnik & Zmaj Hall, Zagreb

The inaugural Atlas Conference established a foundation for critical discourse on modern challenges. Students and scholars gathered to examine the concept of responsibility in the contemporary world, exploring how traditional values intersect with modern complexities. This groundbreaking two-day event set the tone for Atlas's mission to foster intellectual excellence and meaningful dialogue, successfully bringing together leading international and domestic experts.

Thematic Areas

  • Risk and Responsibility in the Contemporary World
  • Dangers and Risks of Technological Development
  • Responsibility in Market Operations
  • Dangers, Risks and Responsibilities in Maintaining Croatian Cultural Uniqueness
